Here's a time line of Eminem's life:


  • 1972 - Born in Detroit, Michigan. He comes from a very poor family
    and his mother was a drug-addict and alchoholic

  • 1989 - Eminem dropped out of Lincoln High School at age 17.

  • 1997 - Eminem went to Los Angeles to compete in the 1997
    Rap Olympics and he placed second.

  • 1999 - He released The Slim Shady LP in February 1999 and become
    a succsessful global artist

  • 2000 - The Marshall Mathers LP was released in May 2000.
    It sold 1.76 million copies in its first week.

  • 2002 - The Eminem Show was released in May 2002.

  • 2004 - Encore is the fifth studio album released by Eminem.
    It was released on November 12, 2004

  • 2006 - Proof, who was Eminem's childhood friend, was murdered,
    leaving him completely devastated

  • 2010 - His seventh album was released on June 18.
    In the US Recovery sold 741,000 copies during its first week.

  • NOWADAYS - He's the biggest legend of rap history, no freaking doubt.
